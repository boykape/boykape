MY BIOGRAPHY

Angelo's exploration of technology began with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, where he showcased outstanding coding skills and an innovative mindset. Within the academic halls of JRMU, Angelo actively contributed to research projects, leaving a lasting impact on the university's computer science community. His commitment to excellence and technological advancement earned him recognition from both faculty and peers. Post-graduation, Angelo took his skills to the tech industry, co-founding a startup dedicated to creating sustainable solutions for businesses. His leadership and dedication to technological excellence propelled the company to success, earning accolades within the industry. Angelo remains connected to his alma mater, engaging in mentorship programs and workshops for JRMU students. His passion for fostering the next generation of tech leaders is a testament to the impact of his education at JRMU. Now, at 25, Angelo Tagob continues to shape the landscape of computer studies, drawing inspiration from his roots at JRMU. His journey serves as an inspiring narrative, illustrating the transformative power of education and innovation in the dynamic field of computer science.
